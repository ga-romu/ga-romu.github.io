MAmiña Church

GetARQ | 2022

This project aimed to survey and create a precise repository of 3D models, photogrammetry, and 2D architectural drawings for a 400-year-old church in the Atacama Desert, that was seriously damaged after an earthquake in 2005 and a fire in 2017.

The information our team has provided makes the restoration team’s mission easier.
